Photograghy

Photography is my life-long passion. I picked it up as a kid, watching my father developing black and white films, roll after roll, moving prints from one solution to enother under the red light.

I was holding my breath, watching - the magic happening right in front of my eyes - familiar faces and places appearing out of nowhere on a little white square of paper.

Since then I am looking at this world through the eye of my camera, following the footsteps of my father.
